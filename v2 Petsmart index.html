The case for embedded teams

Why this model wins
for PetSmart.

Most creative staffing arrangements force a choice between control and flexibility. The SketchDeck embedded model delivers both — talent that shows up, learns your brand, and is managed entirely by us.

🏢

They work in your building

Embedded resources operate on-site at PetSmart's Phoenix HQ four days a week — inside your workflows, tools, and culture from day one. No re-briefing on every project.

⚡

Zero employer overhead

SketchDeck handles all W-2 employment, benefits, HR, performance management, and recruiting. PetSmart gets the creative output without any administrative burden.

🎯

One point of contact

A dedicated Program Manager owns intake, stakeholder updates, resource utilization, and escalations — your team manages outcomes, not vendors.

📈

Scale up or down

Team composition can flex as business needs evolve — roles added, reduced, or respecced by mutual agreement. The program moves with your marketing calendar.

🔒

Rate protection at launch

For transitioning contractors, SketchDeck locks in the same rate and markup structure for the first 12 months. Pricing protected from day one — no surprises.

🔄

Continuity built in

When a team member departs, recruiting is initiated within 3 business days. PetSmart never loses momentum during a transition.

Key terms

How we work together.

Employment Model

Section 8.3

All SketchDeck resources are W-2 employees or authorized subcontractors. PetSmart bears zero employer-of-record liability.

Rate Protection

Section 7

Replacement hires for transitioning contractors locked at the same rate and markup for the first 12 months. Pricing stability guaranteed.

IP Ownership

Section 10.3

PetSmart owns all deliverables produced under this agreement. Your brand assets and creative output are 100% yours.

Termination

Section 8.2

Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice. PetSmart pays only fees accrued through the termination date. No penalties.

Capacity Flexibility

Section 5.2

Team composition adjustable by mutual agreement. Fees adjust accordingly in the next billing cycle within the NTE.

Knowledge Transfer

Section 8.4

All program documentation delivered to PetSmart within 30 days of termination. No knowledge held hostage.
